Who We Are

TK Precision Toolings Limited (TKPT)

Formerly known as Kras Asia Ltd, was acquired by TK Group and re-established on 2002 followed the liquidation of Kras Asia Ltd.

Original equipment manufacturing-OEM

KPT offers a wide variety of encapsulation solutions and techniques from our experienced design team. We offer Conventional, Bottom or Top Transfer Multi-Gang Pot (BTM-MGP / TTMGP) molds for IC, Discrete products...
From Manual System to Semi-Auto to Fully Auto Systems which incorporates Hardware and Software for backend assembly process. Apart from standard machines, TKPT offers flexibility to custom design systems and dies for endless customer design requirement.

Automated machinery

TKPT also offer design, develop and manufacturing automated machinery comprising equipment and sub-assemblies system for other industries such as plastic, polishing material, packaging and etc...
Our automated machinery utilizing metal casting, metal fabrication, electronics assemblies as well as PLC control, we procure pneumatic, electronics and mechanical components from local and overseas suppliers. All of these components will assemble into sub-assemblies, modules or systems as complete machinery, final machine will subject to extensive inspection and testing before shipment to customers.

Precision machinery parts and Framework fabrication

TKPT also make spare chase for Auto mold, spare dies and other critical spare parts from metal, non-metal and composite materials.
also manufacturing complete framework, stainless steel sheet, metal tubing, bend, form and painted sheet metal parts along with wiring will be assembled into complete framework.
